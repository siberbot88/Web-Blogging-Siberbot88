What Is HarvestFarm?

HarvestFarm is a disruptive digital platform meticulously engineered to empower small-scale farmers by enabling direct market access, agricultural education, and transparent financing solutions. It’s not just an app—it’s a movement to dismantle the exploitative grip of middlemen and to revitalize the agricultural economy in Indonesia through smart, sustainable innovation.

Whats a Problem ?

  • Exploitation by Middlemen (Tengkulak)
    Farmers remain trapped in predatory lending cycles, often forced into unfair “ijon” practices where they sell crops at below-market prices just to secure initial capital.
  • Lack of Market Access & Real-Time Price Transparency
    Over 60% of farmers are disconnected from live market data, crippling their ability to negotiate fair prices and access broader sales opportunities.
  • Limited Financing Channels
    With little access to cooperative credit or banks, farmers are often coerced into high-interest, conditional loans, perpetuating poverty.
  • Underutilization of Modern Agricultural Technology
    Less than 20% of farmers leverage digital or modern tools, hindering productivity and efficiency.
  • Price Inflation Hurts Both Sides
    Despite soaring food prices, farmers see minimal gains while consumers bear the burden of an over-extended distribution chain.

The Game-Changing Solution

HarvestFarm delivers a holistic and scalable solution that integrates:

  • Direct Farmer-to-Consumer Marketplace
    Farmers can bypass middlemen and capitalize on fair-market pricing.
  • Real-Time Price Monitoring
    Integrated algorithms analyze market data live, empowering farmers to strategically price their products.
  • Smart Micro-Financing Access
    Collaborations with cooperatives and credit unions allow for low-risk, flexible capital loans, eliminating the need for exploitative practices.
  • Digital Training & Agricultural Education
    Farmers are equipped and upskilled through targeted digital modules on crop management, pricing strategy, and tech adoption.
  • Seamless Payment and Logistics System
    Built-in support for QRIS, e-wallets, bank transfers, and logistics tracking ensures a smooth and secure transaction experience for both farmers and consumers.

What Are the Results?

  • 100% Success Rate in Usability Testing
    Users effortlessly navigated key tasks like sign-up, purchasing, payment, and order tracking, validating the app’s intuitive design.
  • High User Satisfaction (4.4–4.7/5)
    Farmers and buyers praised the platform’s clarity, speed, and value, reflecting strong user retention potential.
  • Operational Efficiency (Tasks Completed in 3–18 Seconds)
    Interactions are fast, responsive, and frictionless, essential for rural adoption.
  • Real Economic Uplift
      Up to 30% increase in farmer income by cutting distribution chain layers
      Affordable prices for consumers, without sacrificing product freshness or quality
      Advancement toward SDGs: Zero Hunger, Decent Work & Economic Growth, and Responsible Consumption

HarvestFarm spearheads a new era of equitable agriculture. It does not merely connect—it cultivates prosperity, fosters independence, and streamlines the entire agricultural value chain through technology and trust. This is more than innovation—it’s agricultural liberation.
