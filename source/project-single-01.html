What is Safetion?

Safetion is a breakthrough mobile application concept designed to map and alert users about high-risk crime zones, particularly areas prone to muggings and street robberies in Surabaya. Developed through a Design Thinking approach, the app focuses on delivering a seamless UI/UX experience while addressing real urban safety challenges.

What Problem Did We Solve?

Surabaya, as one of Indonesia’s major metropolitan cities, is grappling with a surge in violent crimes, particularly muggings. Citizens often feel vulnerable, uninformed, and disconnected from proper reporting channels. Key issues identified include:

  • Lack of real-time awareness about crime-prone locations.
  • Limited accessibility to crime reports and law enforcement channels.
  • Ineffective distribution of public safety information.
  • Absence of community-based digital tools to foster vigilance.

What Solution Does Safetion Offer?

Safetion delivers a user-centric digital safety ecosystem. Its solutions are meticulously engineered to:

  • Visualize Danger Zones : Safetion deploys a real-time heatmap that highlights red (high risk) and yellow (moderate risk) zones using geographic data overlays.
  • Empower Crime Reporting: The E-Lapor (E-Report) feature enables users to file digital reports directly to authorities, enhancing response speed and visibility.
  • Foster Community Awareness: Through E-Sharing, users can exchange real-life experiences, warnings, and insights to collectively combat street crime.
  • Broadcast Trusted Information : A built-in news board ensures up-to-date crime news, with notifications and safety tips that strengthen public preparedness.

What Are the Results?

After several phases of prototyping, user testing, and expert evaluations, Safetion demonstrated remarkable performance:

  • Usability Testing Score: 98/100 (via Maze tools)
  • System Usability Scale (SUS) Score: 85% – categorized as Excellent
  • Positive Heuristic Evaluations from five professional UI/UX designers
  • Key revisions were implemented, such as font optimization, interface consistency, highlighting key features, and improving accessibility for all user segments.

Safetion is not just an app — it’s a digital safety movement. It bridges the gap between citizens, data, and authorities with precision, empathy, and innovation. With its intuitive design, community-focused features, and real-world usability, Safetion is poised to become a transformative force in urban safety technology.
